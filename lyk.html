LYK
Lubaba Yarn Kitchen

Bangladesh's First Transparent
Waste-to-Yarn Ecosystem

From waste to high-value yarn

LYK is our circular yarn initiative, transforming post-industrial textile waste into premium, traceable regenerated yarns for global brands.

The System

A circular system, not just a product

  • Waste-to-yarn transformation — post-industrial textile waste collected, sorted and processed into high-quality regenerated yarn

  • Traceability by design — batch-level tracking from raw waste to finished yarn, DPP (Digital Product Passport) readiness built in

  • Multi-application development — yarns engineered for knitwear, weaving and home textiles across different gauges and constructions

  • Scalable production model — from sample quantities to commercial volumes, designed for real supply chain integration

Context

Why this matters

The global textile industry is shifting toward circular materials, driven by regulation, brand commitments and consumer expectations. LYK positions Fashion Comfort at the centre of this transition — not as a follower, but as an enabler.

  • Value retention — keeping materials in use longer, reducing waste and preserving the value embedded in fibres

  • Reduced dependency — less reliance on external virgin fibre sourcing, more control over the material pipeline

  • EU regulation readiness — aligned with upcoming European textile waste and traceability legislation

  • Upstream control — direct ownership of material inputs, from collection through yarn spinning

Integration

Connected to development and production

LYK is not a standalone initiative — it is directly integrated with our sampling facility and production operations, enabling a seamless path from material innovation to finished garments.

  • Faster experimentation — test new yarn blends and compositions directly in our sample home

  • Yarn-to-garment development — from regenerated yarn to knitted sample in one integrated workflow

  • Quality control — continuous feedback loop between yarn production and garment manufacturing
