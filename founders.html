Our Founders

The people behind Fashion Comfort Group

Md. Afzalur Rahman

Managing Director

Md. Afzalur Rahman leads the overall strategic direction of Fashion Comfort Group with vision and innovation. As Managing Director, he has been instrumental in building the company from its founding in 2002 into an internationally recognised knitwear manufacturing partner.

Under his leadership, the group has expanded operations across multiple countries, established direct production facilities in Bangladesh, and built long-term relationships with leading fashion brands across Europe and Asia. His commitment to quality, reliability and responsible production continues to define the company's approach.

Salma Rahman

Chairman

Salma Rahman oversees financial planning and strategic risk management for Fashion Comfort Group. As Chairman, she provides governance and strategic oversight ensuring the company maintains its financial health and sustainable growth trajectory.

Her expertise in financial management and corporate governance has been essential in navigating the complexities of international textile manufacturing, from managing supply chain economics to ensuring compliance with global trade regulations and sustainability standards.

Sadman Ishraq Hamid

Director

Sadman Ishraq Hamid coordinates the regenerated yarn facility and sustainability initiatives at Fashion Comfort Group. As Director, he drives the company's commitment to circular economy principles and environmental responsibility.

His work on the LYK Project and Lubaba Yarn Kitchen represents the next generation of the company's vision — developing innovative regenerated yarn technology and building transparent waste-to-yarn ecosystems that position Fashion Comfort at the forefront of sustainable textile manufacturing.
