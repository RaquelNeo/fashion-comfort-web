Responsibility

A responsible approach

Responsibility is integrated into our operations and production units,
with a focus on measurable actions and continuous improvement.

Environmental impact

Our operational approach includes practical systems and long-term initiatives designed to reduce environmental impact and support more responsible manufacturing.

Solar energy generation

Solar energy generation of up to approximately 1,000 kWh per day

Water recycling systems

Industrial water recycling systems with a capacity of up to 240,000 liters per day

Material circularity

Recycling processes that convert factory fabric waste into ready-to-use recycled materials

Workforce development

Long-term workforce retention and development programs
